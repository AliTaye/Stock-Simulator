Stock Simulator

A browser-based stock market simulation game that provides a fun and interactive experience to learn the basics of trading and investing. Start with $200 and aim to reach a total net worth of $10,000 by buying and selling stocks smartly!

Game Link:

https://alitaye.github.io/Stock-Simulator/

Key Features

Dynamic Market: Daily price fluctuations based on news and random factors.

Impactful News: A TV screen displaying news that affects entire market sectors.

Diverse Stocks: A variety of companies across different sectors (Tech, Metals, Health, Military, Finance).

New IPOs: New companies are listed every 10 days to increase investment opportunities.

Commission System: A 1% fee (minimum $1) is deducted on every trading transaction.

Company Bankruptcy: If a stock's price drops below $1, the company goes bankrupt, and you lose all your investments in it!

Dividends: Some companies distribute periodic cash dividends to their shareholders.

Black Market: The ability to buy leaked insider information to guarantee a huge profit the next day.

Loan System: Request a loan when bankrupt to get a second chance and continue playing.

Transaction History: Keep track of all your actions, including buying, selling, and profits.

Interactive UI: A modern and attractive design with built-in support for both Arabic and English languages.

Sound Effects: Immersive interactive sounds that enhance the gameplay experience.

How to Play

Watch the News: Keep an eye on the TV screen for news and hints that might impact the market.

Analyze the Market: Monitor stock price trends through the mini sparkline charts.

Trade: Buy or sell stocks based on your analysis and predictions.

End the Day: Click the "End Day" button to advance time, update prices, and reveal new events.

Achieve the Goal: Continue investing wisely until your total wealth reaches $10,000 to win the game!

How to Run

Download the stock-game.html file.

Open the file using any modern web browser (such as Chrome, Firefox, or Safari).

Enjoy playing!

Customization & Development

You can easily modify the stock-game.html file to tweak the game's core values:

INITIAL_BALANCE: Change the starting balance.

TARGET_WEALTH: Change the target amount required to win.

FEE_RATE: Change the trading commission percentage.

You can also add more companies to (INITIAL_STOCKS, IPO_POOL) or custom news to (NEWS_POOL).
